Cakes, cupcakes & more...

Welcome to Melinda's Cakes & Designs!

Born in the Philippines, Melinda moved to San Diego in 2004 away fron the glitz and glamour of Los Angeles. San Diego, a paradise, where she quickly fell in love with it's mild year-round climate and natural deep-water harbor, enjoys snorkling, growing orchids and decorating cakes. As a hobby, she loves to a put smile on your face by making you a gorgeous cake designs. Her motto...

"The noblest art is that of making others smile."

Cake Decorating Classes

Learn the art of cake designs...

Looking for a unique party idea for you and/or your kids? Well, Melinda's Cakes & Designs offers Cake Decorating Classes! What a Super fun experience for kids (and/or adults) to learn while making delicious art! While kid’s birthday parties are most common, they can be held for adults too: birthday parties, showers, business/industry events and more. No matter what the occasion, bring a smile, some friends, and your creativity!
